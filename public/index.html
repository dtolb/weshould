What is it?

Messaging & Context, Together

No more switching apps or worrying about data use.

Weshould allows users of a group message to reference outside information without leaving the message. No more switching from one app to another. Figure out the weather, nearby concerts, or a restaurant with an ongoing deal all in one place. Weshould allows you to stop asking & start doing.

Two Simple Steps to Set It Up

1Get a Number

Text your ZIP code to (978) 272-2388. We'll use this to localize your experience.

You'll receive a text with a new Weshould phone number.

Add your new Weshould number to any group chat.

2Type @Weshould…

…get dinner.     …go to a movie.     …go for a run.

Mention any of Weshould's triggers and it will help make plans, directly in your group chat.
